AI-powered fuzzy matching improves ACH payee name detection compared to traditional string-based algorithms. As of NACHA's June 2026 rules, financial institutions must use risk-based processes to identify unauthorized ACH credits, making robust name matching critical for catching fraud like mule accounts and business email compromise schemes.
