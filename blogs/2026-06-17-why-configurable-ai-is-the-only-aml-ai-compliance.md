The post argues that compliance teams need AI systems they can configure, audit, and explain to regulators, not pre-built black-box models. Unit21's configurable AI approach lets compliance teams define risk factors for their institution, control which investigation tasks run, set automation levels per queue, and specify narrative output, enabling them to adapt to new typologies within days rather than waiting for vendor updates.
